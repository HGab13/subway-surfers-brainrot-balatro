# subway-surfers-brainrot-balatro
Mod for Balatro v1.0.1o

Modded balatro, but there's a Subway Surfers gif playing on the top left of your screen.
There's also a rare chance that a segment of the main theme plays, but what are the odds for that?

## Installation "guide"
- Required: steamodded
- Download the contents found in  ```<>code -> Download ZIP``` and place them in the mods folder for your Steamodded installation

## Also
When hovering over the subway surfers gif, you can see through it until it's almost completely transparent
